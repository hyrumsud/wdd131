Project Site Plan: NicheAI

The purpose of this micro-SaaS is to provide creators, freelancers, and business owners with real trend-based content ideas in just seconds. Users simply enter a niche or industry, and the platform automatically analyzes data from public sources such as Google Trends, YouTube, TikTok, Reddit, and X.

NicheAI helps users save time, stay relevant, and create better content based on real data.

3. Scenarios

  1. How can I quickly find trending content ideas for my niche?

    A creator or freelancer wants fast ideas without spending hours researching trends.

  2. Can this tool help me generate content ideas even if I’m starting a new niche?

    A new business owner testing a niche wants real-time insights to guide their content strategy.

  3. What topics are trending right now in my industry?

    A marketer or entrepreneur wants to know which subjects are currently performing best.

#7C3AED

Aqua / Cyan Accent

Dark Navy

#0F172A

Soft White

Used for body text, cards, layout spacing.

5. Typography

NicheAI uses a modern, clean type system that balances readability and a futuristic look.

Poppins

Use: Headings (H1–H3), titles, navigation

This font gives a soft futuristic and friendly shape to the text, perfect for titles and key interface elements.

Heading Example – Discover real trend-based content ideas.

Inter

Use: Body text, paragraphs, forms, buttons

Inter is clean, modern, and easy to read. It is ideal for paragraphs and long-form content inside the app.

Body Example – Enter your niche and get 3 data-backed ideas instantly.
